The Farm of 38°30° Cheese Factory, Selected as Finalist for City Scape Awards 2017

The Farm of 38° 30° Cheese Factory designed collaboratively by Slash Architects and Arkizon Architects is now a finalist for City Scape Awards in “Commercial Projects” category.
The winners will be announced on the 11th of September after each project’s presentations in Dubai city.
