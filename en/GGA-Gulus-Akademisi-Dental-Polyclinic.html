Gulus Akademisi Dental Polyclinic

program:

Polyclinic, 320m2


location:

Gaziantep, Turkey, 2015


status:

Under Construction

The main attitude of project is to create a visually striking display window which expresses the identity of an aesthetic and plastic clinic via combining impressive architecture through minimal solutions. The “flying box” concept has brought dynamism to the minimal, monochrome and orthogonal design approach. The dynamism is maintained by the expansion of the big box unit – two storeys high– from the existing facade outline. The second box, smaller, on top of the mezzanine inside is also hanging over, complementing the flying box concept. These two boxes of different sizes complement each other both visually and functionally. The contrast between the gridal system of the clinics and the clear entrance facade is maintained allowing us to perceive the depth of the reception, lounge and circulation areas. The optimisation of spaces are re-evaluated by the parameters such as optimum circulation, sunlight and fresh air.
