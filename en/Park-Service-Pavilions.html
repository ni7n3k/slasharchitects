Park Pavilions

program:

Service pavilions


location:

Kağıthane, Istanbul, Turkey, 2013


status:

Turgut Cansever Competition, Honorable mention prize, under construction


in collaboration with:

Batu Kepekçioğlu, Volkan Taşkın

All of the service pavilions that are designed aim to carry out a new approach to new typologies. In addition to the necessary functions that are expected from these pavilions have some additional privileges that improve the quality of life in the existing park. These units such as sports center, sera-café and children center are all produced with contemporary materials and up-to date structural compositions.
