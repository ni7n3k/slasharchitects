The Farm of 38°30°

program:

Dairy factory, 800m2


location:

Afyon, Turkey, 2014


status:

built


in collaboration with:

Arkizon

The Farm of 38°30° is located in Afyon, Tazlar Village. Differencing from the ordinary typology of factory, the building also has a shop and a degustation part. The linear program of the factory is situated on the land by being folded into a circular form, creating an interior courtyard where the visitors can experience and watch the production of cheese. The production area is more transparent on the courtyard site as the background of the production are situated behind the corridor and has more opaque facade. The service entrances and necessities of the technical areas are situated at the back side of the building.
