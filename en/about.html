slasharchitects

Re-thinking various typologies of architecture through urban, operational and spatial potentials; slasharchitects pursuits re-writing the built environment within the urban context. Integrating every scale and area of design, we prioritize a sensitive approach to all.

Combining a number of professions that interfere with architectural design, Slash Architects derives its name from the word “slasher”. Today the word “slasher” points to a structure that embodies various expertise and therefore achieves a more holistic approach to design. As opposed to “one-man-does-all architecture” we aim to generate spaces as a more collaborative work integrating a variety of experiences and skills. Founded by Şule Ertürk Gaucher and İpek Baycan in 2013, under the name of glaister||gaucher architects; we renewed our organization as slasharchitects performing in different scales of design such as urban design, architecture, interiors and construction.

İpek Baycan

Founder

In 2008, she graduated from Istanbul Technical University Faculty of Architecture. Between 2008-2010, she received her Master of Architecture degree from I.T.U. Architectural Design Program where she wrote her thesis “Buffer Place: A New Form of Spatial Occurrence in the Fragmented Metropolitan City.” In this thesis she derived the sociological notion of "Buffer Mechanism" into "Buffer Place" documenting various architectural spaces in the city. Between the years 2010-2012 she worked as an architect for Emre Arolat Architects; involved in projects concerning different scales and typologies of architecture. Later on, she worked in collaboration as a freelance architect with different architectural offices on various projects ranging from urban design to interior design. She worked as a part-time lecturer in design studio of Gebze Technical University and Yıldız Technical University.

In 2013, she founded glaister||gaucher architects with Şule Ertürk Gaucher. Since 2016, the office continues their professional work under their new identity as slasharchitects.

Şule Ertürk Gaucher

Founder

Following her Bachelor’s Degree in Architecture from Yildiz Technical University, between the years 2012-2013 she started her Master’s degree in Computer Aided Design. Later on she joined Ecole Spéciale d’Architecture in Paris where she developed her thesis project concerning computational tools applied to urban design. Starting her architectural career in 2009, she has worked on a variety of scales and programs. Between 2012 and 2013, she worked in the conceptual modelling department of the Ateliers Jean Nouvel. Since 2012, she has been tutoring both at schools and international workshops about Parametric Tools, Advanced Modelling and Generative Design processes. She worked as a part-time lecturer in design studio of Yıldız Technical University.

In 2013, she founded glaister||gaucher architects with İpek Baycan. Since 2016, the office continues their professional work under their new identity as slasharchitects.
