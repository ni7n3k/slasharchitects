slasharchitects

slasharchitects is a studio that pursuits different forms of realizing architecture and uses design as an interface in order to increase different potentials of architecture. One of the main approaches of the studio for design is through critical thinking. slasharchitects was founded in 2014 by İpek B. Glaister and Şule E. Gaucher’s accumulation of experiences in various fields of architecture and design. The studio has a holistic approach to design that aims to re-interpret different typologies of architecture by contemporary architectural production. In addition to the existing Istanbul office, slasharchitects founded their office in Mersin recently. The office sustains their services in architecture, interior architectural In their office for all around Turkey. Maintaining experienced project teams gga is also includes experienced construction teams and coordination in order to get their projects realized perfectly.

İpek Baycan Glaister

Founder of slasharchitects

In 2008, she graduated from Istanbul Technical University, Faculty of Architecture. Between 2008-2010, she received her Master of Architecture degree from I.T.U. Architectural Design Program where she wrote her thesis “Buffer Place: A New Form of Spatial Occurrence in the Fragmented Metropolitan City.” Throughout her studies she participated in various design projects, international architecture competitions, construction projects, exhibitions and publications. She received two mentions, the first in Politecnico di Milano, Compasso Volante International Competition and the second in Kızılay City Square National Urban Design Competition. Between 2010-2012, she worked as an architect in Emre Arolat Architects; involved in projects concerning different scales and typologies of architecture. Later on, she worked as a freelance architect with different architectural offices. Throughout her free-lance career she has worked in collaboration on a variety of projects ranging from urban design to interior design projects. She continues her professional work in slasharchitects which she founded with Şule Ertürk Gaucher in 2014. Together they have received the Kıyı Köşe Caferağa Sport Center Site first prize. Fallowing that they have received honorable mention prizes both in Turgut Cansever National Architecture Competition and Çanakkale War Research Center Competition. Between 2014-2015 she worked as a a part time lecturer in Gebze Institute of Technology for 3rd year Design Studio.

Şule Ertürk Gaucher

Founder of slasharchitects

After 5 years of Bachelors in Architecture in Yildiz Technical University and a year of Master’s degree in Computer Aided Design, she joined the Ecole Spéciale d’Architecture in Paris. She developed a thesis on Parametric Urbanism in YTU concerning computational tools to urban design. Since 2012, she had the chance to tutor both at school and international workshops about Parametric Tools, Computational Design and Generative Design processes. Since 2009, she has been in charge of the projects, from concept design to delivery. She has worked on a variety of scales and programs; mixed-use buildings, skyscrapers, hotels, malls, office spaces, as well as housing and interior architecture for different restaurants. In 2013, she worked in the 3D department of the Ateliers Jean Nouvel. She continues her professional work in slasharchitects which she founded with Ipek Glaister in 2014. Together they have received the Kıyı Koşe Caferaga Sport Center Site first prize. Fallowing that they have received honorable mention prizes both in Turgut Cansever National Architecture Competition and Çanakkale War Research Center Competition.
